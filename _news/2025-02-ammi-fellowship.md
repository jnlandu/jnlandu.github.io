I was awarded a fully funded fellowship to teach in the Machine Learning stream at the [African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS)](https://aims-senegal.org/) for the Machine Learning Master'sprogram (AMMI).

As part of the fellowship, I run bootcamps in Python, PyTorch, Linear Algebra, and Introduction to Machine Learning, and support graduate students with coursework and research questions. It has been a good opportunity to consolidate what I know by teaching it, and to work alongside researchers whose work I respect.
