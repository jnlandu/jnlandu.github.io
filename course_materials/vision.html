Computer Vision and Diffusion Models

This course was split into two parts: the first part was an introduction to computer vision, taught by Dr. Laurens van der Maaten from Meta AI, and co-inventor of t-SNE. The second part focused on object detection and was given by Dr. Natalia Neverova from Meta AI.
